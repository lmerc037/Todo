Sound Studio Setup Checklist

  • Turn on all the SWITCHES (Projector, Screen, track, stage, TVs, Sub) ?
    Ensure all switches for the projector, screen, track, stage, TVs, and subwoofer are turned on.
  • Turn on the POWER AMPLIFIERS inside the white Rack ?
    Power amplifiers are located inside the white rack; make sure they are powered on for proper audio output.
  • Turn on the AUDIO MIXER ?
    Ensure the audio mixer is powered to manage different audio inputs effectively.
  • Activate the FADER FLIP and Increase the MAIN VOLUME to 5 ?
    Activate the fader flip function and adjust the main volume control to the level 5 setting.
  • Now Go DOWNSTAIRS inside the CHURCH ?
    Proceed to the downstairs area of the church for further setup requirements.
  • Make sure that all 4 SPEAKERS inside the church are turned on ?
    Verify that all four speakers located inside the church are powered and functioning.
  • Turn on the STAGE BOX ?
    Ensure the stage box is activated to facilitate various stage connections.
  • CONNECT / TURN ON the Floor Monitor and the TV ?
    Make sure the floor monitor and TV are both connected and turned on for visual and audio monitoring.
